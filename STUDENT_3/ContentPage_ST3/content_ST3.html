Heliogen

California, USA

Heliogen is a renewable energy technology company focused on decarbonizing industry and empowering a sustainable civilization. The company's concentrating solar energy and thermal storage systems aim to deliver carbon-free heat, steam, power, or green hydrogen at scale to support round-the-clock industrial operations.

China

Jinko Solar Co., Ltd. is a globally leading PV module manufacturer and energy storage system integrator.It is the world's largest solar panel manufacturer, producing high-efficiency photovoltaic modules for global deployment.Embracing the mission of "optimizing the energy portfolio and taking responsibility for enabling a sustainable future," the company strategically positions itself in the core segments of the photovoltaic industry chain.

Daqo New Energy

China

Daqo New Energy is a Chinese company engaged in the manufacture of monocrystalline silicon (mono-Si) and polysilicon (poly-Si), primarily for use in solar photovoltaic systems. Founded in 2007, the Company manufactures and sells high-purity polysilicon to photovoltaic product manufactures, who further process the polysilicon into ingots, wafers, cells and modules for solar power solutions. The Company has a total polysilicon nameplate capacity of 305,000 metric tons and is one of the world's lowest cost producers of high-purity polysilicon.

Enpal

Germany

Enpal, a greentech unicorn based in Germany, pioneers the energy transition with its integrated solution for renewable energy.It is a renewable energy equipment company based in Berlin that primarily sells and installs photovoltaic systems and heat pumps. Enpal has seized this moment spectacularly, transforming from a small solar startup to a €905 million revenue powerhouse in just six years. Enpal installs photovoltaic systems, battery storage systems, wallboxes, smart meters, and heat pumps, and offers an intelligent energy management system.

Nuclear

Oklo

USA

Oklo Inc. is an advanced nuclear technology company.Oklo is developing next-generation fission powerhouses to produce abundant, affordable, clean energy at a global scale – starting with the Aurora, which can produce up to 75MWe of electrical power. Oklo's fast reactors incorporate inherent safety features and can be fueled by recycled waste. Oklo made history with the first advanced reactor site use permit, winning access to recycled fuel, and submitting the first ever advanced reactor combined license application.

Constellation

Constellation Energy Corporation is an American energy company that provides electric power, natural gas, and energy management services. Constellation provides electric power to commercial and industrial customers. Its electricity supply business manages energy sales, dispatch, and delivery from Exelon's power generation portfolio to utilities, municipal co-ops, and energy retailers nationwide.

Bloom Energy

USA

Bloom Energy specializes in solid oxide fuel cell technology that converts natural gas or biogas into clean electricity efficiently. Their fuel cells provide on-site power with a significantly lower carbon footprint, helping reduce reliance on fossil fuels and improving grid resiliency.
